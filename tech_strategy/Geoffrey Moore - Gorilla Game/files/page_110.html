End-of-Tornado Corrections
We have seen that during the tornado, the market corrects itself upward to compensate for insufficient expectations. Eventually, however, these expectations do catch up with reality. This normally happens just as the tornado starts to decelerate. Now the market actually goes too far the other way, projecting a tornado out indefinitely just at the moment when in fact it is beginning to fade. As a result, the next set of reports from the sector surprise and disappoint the market, and all the companies in the sector, including the gorilla, go through a "correction."
