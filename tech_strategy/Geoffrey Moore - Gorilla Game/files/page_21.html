The Technology Adoption Life Cycle
This model is the foundation for understanding high-tech market development. It is based on work done by Everett Rogers and his colleagues at Harvard as far back as the late 1950s. It is still the basis for research under the category of diffusion of innovation and continues to provide insights to high-tech managers every year.
The model predicts that whenever a market is exposed to a discontinuous innovation, customers will self-segregate into five different response profiles:
