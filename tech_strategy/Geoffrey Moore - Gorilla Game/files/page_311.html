Our reason for investing in browser and Web-server software is that it clearly is a gorilla game, with only two major candidates in play and the likelihood of a third entering now being relatively small. Security software is a much more fluid situation, and probably we should wait a quarter or two, but book deadlines being what they are, we would rather jump into than stay out of this category. It almost certainly will be a gorilla game, as every previous play in this category has been, the reason in part being perhaps that you cannot have "open" security.
