Ah well, live and learn. You sell now (even though, as it turns out, the stock will rebound some).

If you did not get out of Informix in 1994, perhaps this headline from Computerworld on March 20, 1995, would have given you sufficient stimulus: "Informix fights also-ran image." Now, there is no shame in fighting such an image, but it should make clear to any reader of this book that the company is in no danger of becoming a gorilla, and there-fore should not be part of a gorilla-game portfolio. Therefore, you would have exited Informix at the latest by the end of March 1995.
