Taken all together, these profiles represent the five basic strategies for responding to the opportunity created by a discontinuous innovation. Any one individual might make any one of the five responses, depending on how he or she sees the new technology. That is, real people tend to be early adopters in one area and late adopters in another. In a free market, however, where the mass of people are left to their own devices, these strategies consistently emerge in a self-organizing, highly repeatable pattern of market development. It is this pattern that lies at the heart of the gorilla game, so it behooves the high-tech investor to study it closely.
