First Principles
The first principle of investing is that if you buy shares in a company, they must be worth something. Why?
Well, okay, there is a market for them, be it the NASDAQ or the New York Stock Exchange or whatever, and there are people who will buy your shares back, and so you could say they are worth whatever those people will pay for them. But why are they worth anything to those people? What's going on here?
