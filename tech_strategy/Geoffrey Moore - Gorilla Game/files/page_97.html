Once again, the impact is felt on the vertical axis (returns) onlythe issue of competitive advantage is moot because the impact on all companies is the same. In this case, it is bad. The government is taking away profits that otherwise would have gone to shareholders. This decreases the net present value of future operating profits by the amount inside the diagonally shaded portion of the curve. A decrease in future profits creates less area under the curveless economic value added. The market cap is therefore lowered, and the stock's price goes down.
